**QUESTION 1: List of Commonly Mentioned Features in Accident Reports**

The following features should be listed:

**1\. Driver Behaviour Features**

- Speeding
- Drunk driving (alcohol influence)
- Distracted driving (mobile phone use)
- Reckless driving / dangerous overtaking
- Driver fatigue (sleepiness)

**2\. Environmental Features**

- Rain
- Fog
- Wind
- Poor visibility
- Darkness (night conditions)
- Wet or slippery roads

**3\. Road and Location Features**

- Highway roads
- Urban roads
- Rural roads
- Intersections / junctions
- Traffic congestion

**4\. Time Features**

- Daytime
- Night-time
- Peak hours
- Off-peak hours

**5\. Vehicle Features**

- Brake failure
- Tire condition
- Vehicle type (car, truck, motorcycle)
- Vehicle age

**6\. Accident Severity Indicators**

- Number of vehicles involved
- Injuries
- Fatalities
- Level of damage

**2\. Vehicle Profile Features**

- Type of vehicle

**3\. Usage and Location Features**

- Area (urban or rural)

- Distance travelled

**QUESTION 3: List of Similar Features Between Narratives and Risk Assessment**

The following similarities should be listed:

- Speeding → Driver behaviour risk
- Drunk driving → High-risk behaviour
- Night driving → Time-related risk
- Rain / bad weather → Environmental risk
- Poor road conditions → Location risk
- Vehicle condition → Vehicle risk
- Accident severity → Claims history
